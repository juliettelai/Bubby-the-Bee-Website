Attacking

How Bubby fights her enemies

Use the right and left arrow keys to move Bubby horizontally, and the spacebar to jump. Pressing the spacebar while midair and holding it allows Bubby to float for a few seconds, slowing your descent. If Bubby falls off the platform, she dies and respawns at the start of the level.

Collect pollen to get more attacking capacity to shoot. However, every time Bubby shoots, the remaining pollen goes down. The amount you have left is shown on the top right corner.

Press ‘z’ to shoot enemies. You can also use the spacebar to jump on them. Bubby has three hearts(found at the top left corner) and if she gets hit three times, she dies and respawns at the start of the level.
